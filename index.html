    About us

    The young design duo, Maulik Bhadani and Piyush Nakrani, the founders of PSquare Designs, Ahmedabad; always aspire to thrill their clients with their dedication, innovation and details. They consider that the minute details in every projects make them ready for the next.

    Maulik and Piyush formed PSquare Designs in 2010, in Ahmedabad and surroundings of Gujarat. Now their forte lies in creating unique and fascinating designs and provide their clients with the best services.

    Maulik pursued his Interior Designing studies from the Bharti Vidyapeeth School of Interior Design, Navi Mumbai. During a casual visit to Ahmedabad, Maulik met a young entrepreneur, Piyush. Though Piyush does not belong to design field, his keen interet in design forced him to pair with Maulik. Since then the results were unexpected and PSquare Designs continue to work in full swing.
